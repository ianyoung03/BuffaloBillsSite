What's next for the Bills?

Heading into Week 5, the Bills host the Tennessee Titans at home in
New Era Field. A win here puts them at 2-3, which keeps the possibility
of playoffs within reach.

Rowdy Bills fans force updated security
and safety measures

During home games, fans have been excessively boisterous and disruptive.
Tailgaters have reportedly "gone to far" by drinking, smoking, and
smashing tables by jumping off of them. All these things have lead
to the Bills organization to reinforce security and safety measures.
