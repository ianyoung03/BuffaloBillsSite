Top Gameday Activities

  • Grab Some Wings at Duff's. Finest wings in Buffalo!

  • Go to the Buffalo Zoo

  • Visit the Buffalo City Hall

  • Explore Woodlawn Beach State Park

  • Visit the Albright-Know Art Gallery

Duff's Famous Buffalo Wings

Stop by Duff's before the game for some delicious wings and a great time!
With our large assortment of sauces and wings, we can
provide you the perfect meal!

Buffalo Zoo

At the Buffalo Zoo, meet the monkeys, gorillas, and so many more animals!
A great visit before a football game, and fun for the
whole family!

City Hall

City Hall is where all the business gets done! Explore Buffalo's deep
political roots, and discover the building that governs
Buffalo!

Woodlawn Beach State Park

Woodlawn Beach State Park is a beautiful park, filled with sand,
wildlife, and scenic views. A great stop before or even after
the big game!

Albright-Know Art Gallery

The Albright-Knox Art Gallery is one of the finest in the nation.
Fit with beautiful art and enriching guides, this is a great spot
to discover all sorts of art!
